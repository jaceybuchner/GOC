Are you a Gladiator of Communication?

Introducing a ‘You’ targeted communication platform focused on public speaking, stage presence, and confidence building skills in a collaborative environment

Types of Speeches with examples (Types of Duels!)

  • Persuasive Speeches
    • Intention is to convince your audience of something
    • You should have a stance or an opinion about your topic
    • All of your strategies should focus on convincing the audience
  • Informative Speeches
    • Intention is to inform your audience about your topic
    • Your speech should be well-researched, and answer why it is important

Prevent Presentation Anxiety/Ways to fight the beast of public speaking

  • Prepare thoroughly
    • Students will have to give multiple speeches and will get used to the preparation process
    • Students will have to outline speeches and rehearse before giving speeches
  • Start Strong
    • Be loud when starting! Give yourself room to stagger your speech
    • Maintain constant breathing and allow yourself to pause if nervous
