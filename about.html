Are you a Gladiator of Communication?

Introducing a ‘You’ targeted communication platform focused on public speaking, stage presence, and confidence building skills in a collaborative environment

Every participant possesses a communication sword, equipped with their own strengths and weaknesses.

Gladiators of Communication learn to sharpen their communication swords and wield them with poise and precision.

Types of Speeches with examples (Types of Duels!)

  • Ø Persuasive Speeches
    • Ø Intention is to convince your audience of something
    • Ø You should have a stance or an opinion about your topic
    • Ø All of your strategies should focus on convincing the audience
  • Ø Informative Speeches
    • Ø Intention is to inform your audience about your topic
    • Ø Your speech should be well-researched, and answer why it is important

Prevent Presentation Anxiety/Ways to fight the beast of public speaking

  • Ø Prepare thoroughly
    • Ø Students will have to give multiple speeches and will get used to the preparation process
    • Ø Students will have to outline speeches and rehearse before giving speeches
  • Ø Start Strong
    • Ø Be loud when starting! Give yourself room to stagger your speech
    • Ø Maintain constant breathing and allow yourself to pause if nervous
