Gladiator Roles

  1. Gladius MC
    • Ø In charge of the session
    • Ø Creates smooth transitions between the gladius presenters
  2. Gladius Presenter
    • Ø Presents prepared and/or impromptu speeches in a friendly collaborative setup
    • Ø Slays communication
  3. Gladius Topics
    • Ø Prepares topics to be presented by Gladius Presenter
    • Ø Comes up with table topics for a surprise impromptu speech
  4. Gladius of Vocabulary
    • Ø Introduces new words and usage to the group
    • Ø Notes mispronunciations, grammatical errors, and incomplete sentences
    • Ø Also counts the number of times the new word was used in communication by the fellow gladiators
    • Ø Helps other gladiators slay communication
  5. Gladius Slayer: Filler-Word Slayer
    • Ø Records any filler words like 'like..', 'And..', 'Um..', 'Ah', ''You Know..' etc used by the Gladius Presenter
    • Ø Prevents the Gladius Presenters from distracting their audience
  6. Gladius of Time
    • Ø Keeps track of the time taken to present by each Gladius Presenter
  7. Gladius Evaluator
    • Ø Evaluates the speech and provides recommendations
